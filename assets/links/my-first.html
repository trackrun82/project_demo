How to make a sandwich

  1. Retrieve all ingredients: 2 slices bread, mustard, turkey, cheese
  2. Spread mustard on 1 slice of bread
  3. Place turkey on top of mustard
  4. Place cheese on top of turkey
  5. Place 2nd slice of bread on top of turkey
  6. Enjoy sandwich!
